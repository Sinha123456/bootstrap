Simple Collapsible 1

Click the button to toggle between showing and hiding content.

The .collapse class indicates a collapsible element(a div in our example); this is the content that will be shown or hidden with a click of a button. To control(show/hide) the collapsible content, add the data-toggle= "collapse" attribute to an "a" or a "button" element. then add the data-target="#id" attribute to connect button with the collapsible content.
